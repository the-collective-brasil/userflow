DIE reflection — three guided stages
D
Describe
GPT guides teacher to produce an objective, factual description. One question at a time. Coaches away from interpretation.
I
Interpret
GPT guides a tentative developmental interpretation. References exposure phase and calibration notes silently. Suggests 2–3 relevant LO codes.
E
Evaluate
For each LO code, GPT guides teacher to assign level 1–5 with a rationale, using rubric descriptors from group profile.
GPT-guidedConversation is structured but not scripted. GPT adapts based on what the teacher describes. Rubric descriptors referenced silently — teacher never sees raw spreadsheet data.
